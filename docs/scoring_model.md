# Scoring Model

## Lighthouse Score

Lighthouse Score measures career fit for Chenshuo's long-term leadership-oriented path.

It is not an ATS score.

| Dimension | Weight |
| --- | ---: |
| Problem Solving | 25 |
| Cross Functional | 15 |
| Customer Exposure | 15 |
| Leadership Growth | 15 |
| AI/Data | 10 |
| Biology | 10 |
| Technical Skills | 10 |

## Interpretation

- 85-100: Tier S. Must apply if sponsorship is not blocked.
- 75-84: Tier A. Strong apply.
- 65-74: Tier B. Strategic reach or apply if market conditions justify volume.
- 55-64: Tier C. Watchlist or skill-gap target.
- Below 55: Pass unless there is a specific strategic reason.

## Hard Pass Conditions

- Explicitly requires US citizenship or green card.
- Explicitly says no current or future sponsorship.
- Requires PhD when the JD clearly treats PhD as a hard requirement.
- Primarily wet lab research with no leadership, customer, product, data, or systems exposure.
- LC-MS, protein purification, animal work, or similar specialized wet-lab requirements when they are central to the role.
- Pure publication-driven research path with limited transition value.
