# Career Tracks

## Track 1: Technical Applications / Scientific Support

Entry roles:

- Technical Applications Scientist
- Scientific Support Specialist
- Technical Support Scientist
- Customer Success Scientist
- Field Applications Scientist

Leadership path:

Technical Applications -> Scientific Solutions -> Product or Support Leadership -> Director

## Track 2: Scientific Solutions / Implementation

Entry roles:

- Scientific Solutions Engineer
- Implementation Scientist
- Bioinformatics Implementation Consultant
- Professional Services Consultant
- Solutions Consultant

Leadership path:

Implementation -> Solutions Architect -> Technical Program / Product -> Director

## Track 3: AI for Biology

Entry roles:

- Applied AI Scientist
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Research Engineer
- Scientific AI Engineer
- BioAI Engineer
- Foundation Model Engineer

Leadership path:

Applied AI -> AI Platform -> AI Product / Technical Lead -> Head of AI or Director

## Track 4: Scientific Software / Platform

Entry roles:

- Scientific Software Engineer
- Bioinformatics Platform Engineer
- Workflow Engineer
- Pipeline Engineer
- Scientific Data Platform Engineer

Leadership path:

Platform -> Solutions / Product -> Engineering or Product Leadership

## Track 5: Clinical Genomics

Entry roles:

- Clinical Genomics Analyst
- Variant Analyst
- Clinical Bioinformatics Analyst
- Reporting Scientist
- Genomic Curation Scientist

Leadership path:

Clinical Analyst -> Senior Variant Scientist -> Scientific Affairs / Clinical Product -> Program or Medical Affairs Leadership

## Track 6: Scientific Product

Entry roles:

- Scientific Product Specialist
- Technical Product Specialist
- Product Analyst
- Associate Product Manager
- Product Operations Associate

Leadership path:

Product Specialist -> Product Manager -> Senior PM -> Product Director

## Track 7: Digital Manufacturing / Manufacturing Systems

Entry roles:

- Manufacturing Systems Engineer
- MES Specialist
- Process Analytics Scientist
- Manufacturing Data Scientist
- MSAT Associate
- Digital Manufacturing Specialist

Leadership path:

Manufacturing Systems -> Automation / MSAT -> Program or Operations Leadership -> Site or Strategy Leadership
